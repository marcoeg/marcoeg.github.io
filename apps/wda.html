WDA - Venture Building Partner

WDA's business involves creating and managing new ventures in all aspects, focusing on the early stage of startups. They act as an experienced Co-Founder, working alongside entrepreneurs to validate projects and launch them into the market. Their mission is to transform projects into sustainable businesses with rapid and effective execution.

WDA aims to position itself in a market segment that is currently underserved, specifically targeting SMEs and entrepreneurs who are seeking innovation but are not yet served by existing companies. They focus on providing business consulting services to startups, entrepreneurs, and SMEs interested in innovating or creating new products and services.
